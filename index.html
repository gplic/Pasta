PASTA WITH SAUSE

LETS LEARN HOW TO MAKE PASTA WITH MUSHROOM SAUSE

INGREDENTS

PASTA

  • PASTA
  • WATER

SAUSE

  • GROUND BEEF
  • ONIONS
  • MUSHROOMS
  • MUSHROOMSOUP
  • WATER
  • GARLIC
  • BLACK PEPPER

INSTRUCTIONS

PASTA

  1. FILL A POT WITH WATER.
  2. BOIL WATER.
  3. ONCE WATER IS BOILING ADD PASTA.
  4. WHEN PASTA IS COOKED STRAIN WATER OUT.

SAUCE

  1. FRY GROUNG BEEF IN A SECOND POT.
  2. CHOP ONIONS AND FRY WITH THE GROUND BEEF.
  3. ONCE GROUND BEEF IS FULLY COOKED ADD MUSHROOMS.
  4. ADD MUSHROOM SOUP AND WATER.
  5. ADD SPICES TO TASTE AND MIX.
  6. ADD SAUSE TO PASTA.
  7. EAT.
